Welcome To Biotech Partners

We Help High School Students for Internship in Biotech Companies.

Biotech Partners is currently at Berkeley & Oakland Tech High, & Peralta Colleges.

Establishing partnerships between the biotechnology industry and school districts since 1993.

Biotech Partners puts science into the hands of students with a biotech-focused curriculum, job training & paid internships.

Biotech Academy students have a 100% graduation rate.

Making a difference through Biotech training.

"I was impressed with the student's ability to quickly come up to speed on the strain-typing project." --Co-op Supervisor, Alta Bates Medical Center.
