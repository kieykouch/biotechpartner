OUR 2016 BRAVO! EVENT IS UNDERWAY!

Join us at Berkeley High School on Saturday, August 13 th to celebrate our 5 th annual BRAVO! Event.

BRAVO! is an exhibition of knowledge and a celebration of youth development. We kick-off this celebration with the science poster competition of Biotech Partners’ high school Biotech Academy summer interns – nearly 70 students – as well as an awards ceremony for the interns and young professionals who have completed our community college programs. We also take this opportunity to recognize and thank our funders, intern hosts and mentors, such as you, and other partners.

Support Biotech Partners and register to attend the event here: https://www.eventbrite.com/e/biotech-partners-2016-bravo-celebration-tickets-26336368788

If you are interested in volunteering, contact Biotech Partners at BPinfo@biotechpartners.org
For more information on sponsorship, please contact our office at 510-705- 7517.
