Becoming a Sponsor

Bay Area biomedical/biotechnology companies and institutions realize significant benefits by partnering with Biotech Partners.
Biotech Partners' programs develop an entry-level biotechnology workforce that is essential to this rapidly growing industry.

You Can Help!

Impact


Industry sponsors, volunteers and mentors are indispensable to the success of Biotech Partners programs. By welcoming co-op or intern students into their laboratories, sponsors, volunteers and mentors benefit from the students´ contributions to their team. But more importantly, mentors recognize that they are contributing to the professional development of a young person who is experiencing his or her first workplace experience. Sponsors, volunteers and mentors receive significant public recognition by Biotech Partners, and are recognized internally for their contributions in meeting their companies' professional and community service objectives.

The Mentor´s Role


Mentors who volunteer with Biotech Partners contribute their time and provide assistance in curriculum development and technical support for the program. This includes working with Biotech Partners' staff to develop a work plan for each student, and receiving guidance and support from Biotech Partners' staff. Mentors often discover that helping a young person get a start in the working world is rewarding both personally and professionally-particularly if the Biotech Partners' graduate is later hired at the mentor's organization.

Appreciated Securities

Additionally, you may be able to deduct the full current market value of the security as a charitable contribution on your income tax return.
