BRAVO! 2016
Our 5th annual BRAVO! Event. Celebrated Biotech Partners student at Berkeley High School on Saturday, August 12th, 2016

BRAVO! 2016 event underscored our success in launching the Biotech Academy in our expansion sites and connecting with over 30 new industry-based internship hosts to support our 2016 Summer Internships Program.   66 Students celebrated the completion of Biotech Partners’ 2016 Summer Internships, with our first cohort of students from our expansion public school high schools, Antioch and South San Francisco, joining the ranks of our legacy schools, Berkeley and Oakland Tech.  

51 Volunteers (including industry partner employees) from the Greater Bay Area regions with science, medical, public health, law, IT and business backgrounds served as judges to select the awardees for the following categories: Best (Overall- includes all the other 3 categories), Most Educational, Most Creative, and Best Oral Presentation. Berkeley and Antioch high school students were awarded and recognized for the categories.

We also take this opportunity to recognize and thank our funders, intern hosts and mentors, such as you, and other partners.

Posters and Graduation 2010
On August 17, 2010 Biotech Partners honored the interns and graduates of the Biotech Academy classes at Berkeley High School and Oakland Technical High School.
